# Sample Facility Editorials — New Format

## Facility 1: Well-Documented Chain Branch

**Mintygreen Home — Subang Jaya, Selangor**

**What it is**
Mintygreen is a mid-sized nursing home in Subang Jaya run by Mintygreen Care, a 7-branch chain. Provides long-term residential care with specialisation in dementia support and post-acute rehabilitation.

**Care services**
- Long-term nursing care
- Dementia & memory care
- Post-operative recovery
- Stroke rehabilitation
- Physiotherapy and occupational therapy (on-site)
- Palliative care support

**Staffing & support**
- Registered nurses on-site 24/7
- Physiotherapists and occupational therapists available
- Languages: English, Malay, Mandarin, some Cantonese
- Caregiver-to-resident ratio: 1:4 (day), 1:6 (night)

**Capacity & pricing**
- 58 beds (mixed occupancy)
- RM 2,400–3,800/month depending on care level and room type
- Pricing published on Mintygreen website (chain-wide rates; final fee depends on assessed care needs)

**License & verification**
- **JKM status:** Registered
- **JKM verified via:** JKM Selangor district office call
- **JKM expires:** August 2027
- **MOH status:** Licensed (Act 586, chain-wide)

**Special programs**
- Respite care: flexible 2-week to 3-month stays
- Day care: for mobile elders; popular for family respite
- Post-stroke rehab: structured 8-week program with therapy frequency documented

**What to ask on visit**
- Doctor visit frequency and on-call availability
- Physiotherapy program structure (frequency, progression, outcome tracking)
- Family visiting hours and involvement in care planning
- Medication management protocols
- How the home manages dementia behaviour and communication

## Facility 2: Small Home, Limited Information

**Cozzi Home Care — Johor Bahru**

**What it is**
Cozzi Home Care is a small residential care home in Johor Bahru serving mobile to low-dependency elders. Focuses on safe housing, daily activities, and meals rather than intensive medical care.

**Care services**
- Residential care (assisted living)
- Basic nursing support
- Meal preparation (halal)
- Daily activities and recreation

**Staffing & support**
- Caregiver staff on-site; nurse availability to be confirmed
- Languages: Malay, English
- Staffing ratio: to be verified on visit

**Capacity & pricing**
- Bed count not published
- RM 1,500–2,200/month (shared to private room)
- Pricing: confirm during visit whether this includes all meals and basic care

**License & verification**
- **JKM status:** Unverified
- **JKM verified via:** None yet (pending outreach)
- **JKM expires:** Unknown
- **MOH status:** Not applicable (does not provide nursing-level care)

**Special accommodations**
- Halal-certified meals
- Muslim-friendly environment
- Prayer room available

**What to ask on visit**
- JKM registration number and current certificate
- Nursing staff availability (registered nurse on-site or on-call?)
- Caregiver qualifications (training certifications, experience)
- Medical oversight (doctor visits, emergency protocols, nearest hospital)
- What is included in quoted monthly fee (meals, laundry, activities, medications)
- Current occupancy and bed availability
- Family visiting hours and phone contact policy
