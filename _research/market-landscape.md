## 4. Verified counts (late 2025 – March 2026)

- **MOH-licensed nursing homes: 18 nationwide** (October 2025 parliamentary data). The only facilities legally recognised as *clinical* nursing homes — IV, stoma, advanced wound care under strict clinical governance.
- **JKM-registered care centres: 393** (Pusat Jagaan Warga Emas, for-profit + NGO, early 2026). Operate under welfare licence, not clinical.
- **Total registered: 411.**

### Why some sources cite ~494
Broader aggregations include "Integrated Facilities" or centres transitioning between Act 506 and the newer **Act 802 (Private Aged Healthcare Facilities and Services Act)**.

### The "shadow" market
SEDAR Institute and March 2026 healthcare journal estimates: **700–1,000 unregistered "backyard" homes** still operate in residential bungalows, concentrated in Johor Bahru and Penang.

### Why MOH counts are so low
Act 586 demands corridor widths, air-exchange rates, high RN ratios that residential settings struggle to meet. Many high-end facilities take the easier Act 506 (JKM) route even when offering nursing services — creating a regulatory grey zone.

## Implications for next phase

1. **Our directory (~350 live) is not exhaustive vs. the registered total of 411** — likely ~85% coverage of registered Klang Valley + Johor; gap is in Penang, Perak, East Malaysia, Negeri/Melaka. Next-phase data work should target these.
2. **Brand-level comparison content has clear demand** — premium families compare Sunway Sanctuary vs Pacific vs Jasper. Consider a dedicated "premium operators compared" guide.
3. **Licence-status labelling matters** — only ~4% of registered facilities are MOH-licensed nursing homes, so the MOH-Licensed badge is genuinely scarce and worth surfacing.
4. **"Backyard" homes are real** — 700–1,000 unregistered facilities means our verification timestamp + licence-status field is a meaningful trust signal, not just compliance theatre.

## Caveats before publishing

- Verify each brand's claimed locations and capabilities individually (these are aggregator claims, not primary verification)
- Cross-check the 18 / 393 figures against MOH parliamentary replies and the JKM directory directly before citing on the live site
- Don't publish the "700–1,000 backyard homes" figure without finding the SEDAR or journal source
