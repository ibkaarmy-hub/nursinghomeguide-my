# Email — first-touch outreach to operator

Formal version of the WhatsApp template. Use when only an email contact is on file, or when the operator's website asks for written enquiries.

Placeholders:
- `<facility-name>` — facility name
- `<facility-url>` — canonical `/facility/<slug>/` URL
- `<form-url>` — pre-filled Google Form link
- `<helper-name>` — your name + role

---

**Subject:** Verifying your facility profile on NursingHomeGuide.my

Dear `<facility-name>` team,

I hope this email finds you well. My name is `<helper-name>` and I'm writing on behalf of NursingHomeGuide.my, Malaysia's independent nursing home and assisted living guide. The site is built and maintained by a team of researchers and clinicians to help families navigate the elder-care landscape based on verified facts, not advertising.

We have published a profile for your facility at:
`<facility-url>`

To ensure the information families see is accurate and current, we would like to invite you to confirm a short list of facts about `<facility-name>` — licensing status, staff coverage, services offered, and pricing model. It takes about 5 minutes to complete.

Once you submit, your profile will display a *"Verified — operator-confirmed"* badge with today's date. Any documents you choose to share with us privately (e.g. licence copies) remain private; we do not republish licence numbers or operator documents.

Verification form (pre-filled with your facility name and address):
`<form-url>`

If you would prefer to answer by email reply, please let me know and I will send the questions in this thread.

Thank you for taking the time. I am happy to answer any questions about how the site works or what verification means.

Warm regards,

`<helper-name>`
Verification team, NursingHomeGuide.my

---

## Helper notes (do not send)

- Use the operator's official email if listed on their site / Facebook
- Do not BCC anyone
- Reply-all only if the operator initiated a thread with multiple recipients
- Save sent copy as PDF/screenshot to `<slug>/operator-correspondence/email-<date>.png` per SOP §3 step 6
