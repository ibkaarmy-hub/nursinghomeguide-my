# Email — first-touch outreach to operator

Use when only an email contact is on file, or when the operator's website directs enquiries to email.

Placeholders:
- `<facility-name>` — facility name as it appears on the public profile
- `<facility-url>` — canonical `/facility/<slug>/` URL
- `<form-url>` — pre-filled Google Form link for this facility
- `<helper-name>` — your full name

**Subject:** Verification invitation — `<facility-name>` on NursingHomeGuide.my

Dear `<facility-name>` team,

NursingHomeGuide.my is an independent elder care directory used by Malaysian families researching nursing home and assisted living options. Your facility has a published profile at:

`<facility-url>`

We are writing to invite you to verify the information on your profile. **Verification is free and takes approximately five minutes.**

---

**What verification involves**

A short online form covering your facility's licensing, staffing, care capabilities, and pricing. You confirm only what is accurate — partial responses are welcome, and you may skip any question.

**What you receive**

Your profile will display a *"Verified — operator-confirmed"* badge with the submission date. Families consistently cite verification status when shortlisting facilities, and the badge signals to them that the information has been reviewed by your team directly.

**How we handle your information**

All responses are attributed on your public profile as *"self-reported by the operator"* with the submission date. Any licence documents you choose to share with us privately for a higher verification tier are stored securely and never republished.

## Helper notes (do not send)

- Use the facility's official email address — from their website, Facebook, or Google Maps listing
- Do not BCC anyone
- Do not copy-paste the "Helper notes" section — this block is internal only
- Save a copy of the sent email (screenshot or PDF) to `<slug>/operator-correspondence/email-<YYYY-MM-DD>.pdf`
- If the operator replies with questions about what the site is or who runs it: "NursingHomeGuide.my is an independent directory built and maintained by a team of researchers and clinicians. We do not accept payment from facilities, and verification is entirely free."
- If they ask about advertising or paid placement: "We don't offer paid placement. Verification is free and affects only the badge on your existing profile."
