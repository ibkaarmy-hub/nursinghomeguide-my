
========== pusat-jagaan-dan-rawatan-orang-tua-al-ikhlas ==========
Pusat Jagaan dan Rawatan Orang Tua Al-Ikhlas is set inside a converted endowed mosque (masjid lama diwakafkan) at Lot 3530, Jalan Batu 18, Kampung Pulau Meranti, Puchong Ś a kampung enclave tucked between Puchong's commercial sprawl and the Putrajaya wetlands. The centre was founded in 1999 by Muji binti Hj Sulaiman, a former Chief Nurse at Hospital Serdang who brought home elderly patients discharged without family to receive them and has been running the home ever since. That clinical background Ś two decades of geriatric ward nursing before founding the home Ś is the most substantive credential in the public record. The facility currently cares for around 50 residents and employs approximately 12 staff.

The centre is Islamic in character, accepting donations of zakat and fitting its care philosophy within an Islamic welfare framework, though available sources do not state that admission is exclusively restricted to Muslims. Care provision includes daily personal assistance (bathing, dressing, feeding), basic medical monitoring, and social and recreational activities with a physical, mental, and spiritual dimension. Reviewer feedback on directory sites praises effective communication between staff and families and a holistic care approach.

The absence of any published pricing, bed availability, or formal JKM licence number means families must visit and ask directly. The converted mosque setting is unconventional and worth seeing in person Ś for some families, the quiet kampung environment and community character will feel exactly right; others may want a more medically equipped facility. Given the 50-resident scale and 12-person staff, confirm the nursing cover arrangement Ś specifically whether trained nurses or caregivers manage clinical tasks overnight, and what the protocol is for medical emergencies given the rural access point.

========== sunstar-golden-care-centre ==========
Sunstar Golden Care Centre is listed as a nursing home in Bandar Sunway, one of Selangor's most densely served elder care corridors given its proximity to Sunway Medical Centre and a large residential population. The facility holds a 5-star Google rating across 24 reviews Ś a strong signal of resident and family satisfaction for a centre of this size Ś but has no published website, Facebook page, or other online presence beyond its Google Maps listing, making independent verification of services difficult.

Bandar Sunway's care landscape is competitive: families in this corridor can choose from Sunway Sanctuary (the premium Sunway Group retirement living product) down to smaller community homes. Sunstar's positioning within this market is unclear from available information Ś it is not featured in aggregator roundups of the top Subang Jaya nursing homes, which may reflect its smaller scale or newer establishment. The phone number (+60 14-660 1127) and its Google presence confirm it is operational, and the rating suggests a positively regarded environment.

Given the absence of public information, an in-person visit is the only reliable way to assess Sunstar Golden Care Centre. When visiting, ask about JKM registration and licence status, the range of dependency levels accepted (low, medium, high, bed-bound), nurse-to-resident ratios, visiting doctor arrangements, and current pricing. Confirm whether the 5-star rating reflects a stable and established operation or a relatively small review pool from early months of operation.

========== pusat-jagaan-rumah-warga-tua-cahaya ==========
Pusat Jagaan Rumah Warga Tua Cahaya is a registered care home situated at No. 2, Jalan Merpati 8, Puchong Jaya, 47100 Puchong, Selangor. Directory sources indicate it has been in operation since 2010 and is classified as an associations-type care provider rather than a corporate nursing home chain. The Google listing attributes a 4.5-star rating from 15 reviews and lists a total bed count of 76, which, if accurate, would make it a mid-sized facility relative to the Puchong neighbourhood home market. The listed phone number is 03-8071 0232.

The facility is described in public directories as offering day-care on a daily basis and full residential care on a monthly arrangement. With 76 beds on record, the home likely accommodates a mix of dependency levels, but nothing about specific nursing capabilities, medical visit frequency, or clinical services has been independently published. No website or active social media page was found during research. The 'associations' classification suggests the home may carry a welfare or community-care mandate rather than operating as a fully commercial nursing operator.

Families considering this home should verify the 76-bed figure and current occupancy directly, and ask what proportion of residents are ambulant versus bed-bound. Given the absence of any digital presence, an in-person visit is essential to assess staffing ratios, hygiene standards, and the quality of the outdoor or communal areas. Clarify whether any trained nurses are on duty overnight, and whether the facility holds a current JKM (Social Welfare Department) licence.

========== gam-yam-senior-home-in-ampang-kuala-lumpur-malaysia ==========
Gam Yam Senior Home operates from 327, Jalan 16, Kampung Baru Ampang Ś a long-established Chinese residential neighbourhood in Ampang that sits on the Selangor-KL boundary. The facility's Google listing categorises it as a retirement home, assisted living facility, and home health care service, suggesting it spans both residential and outreach care. A directory listing places physiotherapy and residential care for the elderly and disabled among its stated activities. The address is shared or closely associated with another entity listed as AIM Healthcare at the same premises, which warrants clarification on who operates the facility and under what licence.

With a Google rating of 3.3 from 11 reviews Ś the lowest in this batch Ś Gam Yam has a materially weaker satisfaction signal than neighbouring facilities in the Ampang area. No website or Facebook presence was found, and no details about capacity, staffing, pricing, or the scope of nursing care are publicly available. The data field for languages listed as '50' in the source data appears to be a data entry error rather than a meaningful value. The Kampung Baru Ampang setting is a densely built residential area with limited outdoor space, though Hospital Ampang is within a few kilometres.

The combination of a below-average rating, absence of any public-facing information, and an unresolved address overlap with another entity means this home warrants careful due diligence before considering placement. Families should request the facility's Care Centre Act licence number, ask about the relationship to AIM Healthcare at the same address, confirm which conditions are managed versus referred out, and seek references from current families. A visit during a mealtime or activity period will give a clearer picture of day-to-day conditions than a formal tour.
