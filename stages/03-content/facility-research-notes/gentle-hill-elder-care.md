## Positioning

Community-focused nursing home in Kulai with a strong emphasis on safety technology, trial admission, and a homely environment. Appeals to families seeking a smaller, more personal facility outside of central JB.

---

## Visiting Hours (distinctive policy)

**Appointment-only** — 3 visiting slots daily (morning / afternoon / evening — confirm exact times). This is a departure from open visiting and has clinical rationale (infection control, schedule stability for residents) but requires families to plan ahead.

## Trial Admission

## Social Worker Notes

**Strengths:**
- 2-week refundable trial is extraordinary — shows confidence in quality and removes risk for families
- Appointment-only visiting with 3 slots shows structure (clinical rationale: reduces disruption to care schedule)
- Fall detection technology = proactive safety investment, not reactive
- VOC-free materials = thought has gone into the physical environment for senior health
- Muslim + vegetarian friendly = inclusive dietary policy
- Hospital Kulai proximity is good emergency coverage for a smaller facility
- Smaller, community-style facility typically means more personal caregiver attention

**Gaps / Watch Points:**
- Only 2-bed and 6-bed rooms — no private option confirmed; families seeking privacy have limited choice
- Clinical capabilities are light compared to EHA Parkview or Jeta Care — may not be suitable for high-dependency patients
- Pricing not published — families can't budget without calling
- Appointment-only visiting may feel restrictive to families who want to drop in freely
- Kulai location means less accessible for KL-based families; JB/Singapore families closer

**Red flags to investigate:**
- "Fall detection technology" — what specifically? Wearable? Bed sensor? Motion camera? This affects privacy significantly — clarify with facility
- No confirmed clinical capabilities for tube feeding, dementia, etc. — must clarify before placing a patient with complex needs

**Checklist for families:**
1. Ask what "fall detection technology" is specifically (wearable vs camera vs sensor)
2. Confirm visiting slot times and what happens if you need to visit outside them
3. Ask about trial admission terms in writing — what exactly is refundable
4. Clarify room capacity — any 2-bed rooms available at intake?
5. Confirm nursing capabilities: can they manage catheters, NGT, dementia?
6. Ask about doctor visit schedule
7. Request JKM licence number
