## Social Worker Notes

**Strengths:**
- Hotel-in-nursing-home concept: genuine private studio rooms (400 sqft) with balconies — exceptional dignity and quality of life vs shared wards
- Gleneagles Medini is 3 min away — best hospital access of any JB nursing home reviewed
- Monthly Singapore outings (Botanic Gardens) = high-quality activities programme; shows investment in resident experience
- Free weekly hotel buffet breakfast = unusual hospitality touch that improves daily life
- Swimming pool + aqua aerobics = one of few nursing homes with an actual therapeutic pool
- Parent company Mintygreen established since 2017 — gives operational credibility
- TCM add-ons (acupuncture, Tui Na) available in-house — good for holistic care model
- Singapore-proximity marketing suggests awareness of cross-border families' needs

**Gaps / Watch Points:**
- **Zero published pricing** — for premium positioning this creates suspicion; Singapore-equivalent pricing would be RM 6,000–12,000+/month, which is a significant barrier
- **JKM licence not visible** — for a care facility with dependent residents this is a serious gap; cannot safely list without confirming
- **5.0/40 reviews** — perfect score at low review count is a concern; newer facility may not have enough candid reviews yet
- **Dementia / high-dependency not confirmed** — the hotel concept may mean they've designed for independent-to-assisted, not frail nursing residents
- **Weekday-only admin** — families with emergencies on weekends may find admin support limited
- **Halal not confirmed** — critical for Malay-Muslim families; Ramada hotel dining may or may not be halal

**Red flags:**
- Perfect 5.0 score at 40 reviews with no third-party validation needs monitoring
- JKM licence absence is a regulatory red flag; verify before any family referral
- "Hotel rooms remodelled" — must confirm fire safety, egress, and medical infrastructure were upgraded to healthcare standards (not just aesthetic)

**Checklist for families:**
1. **Confirm JKM licence number** — ask for the certificate; verify on JKM portal
2. Confirm halal status of Ramada restaurant
3. Get all-in monthly rate in writing (studio / shared)
4. Confirm deposit amount and exit notice period
5. Ask: is the doctor physically on-site 24/7 or on-call?
6. Ask about dementia capability — they may refer out for cognitively impaired residents
7. Ask about staff language (Mandarin / BM / English / Tamil)
8. If Singapore family: ask about admission process for non-Malaysian residents
9. Request AGECOPE documentation
10. Visit on a weekday and ask to see an occupied room (with permission)
