## Social Worker Notes

**Strengths:**
- MemoryCare Home is a genuine clinical differentiator — most nursing homes lack a proper dementia unit
- SmartPeep AI monitoring provides safety assurance for families who can't visit frequently
- Organic farm and animals give residents meaningful activity — strong quality-of-life indicator
- Campus / integrated model means residents can age-in-place as needs change

**Gaps / Watch Points:**
- Pricing from third-party only — not verified with facility; confirm before publishing
- Foreign resident deposit of 3 months is high — note for Singapore-based families
- "Wellness village" positioning may mean lighter clinical staffing vs a pure nursing home
- Need to confirm: is MemoryCare Home licensed as a separate wing? What are the staffing ratios there?
- JKM licence number not captured — essential for dementia unit especially

**Red flags to investigate:**
- Third-party pricing at RM 3,500–4,300 — ask facility to confirm (could be outdated)
- AI monitoring is a selling point but not a substitute for nursing ratio — confirm staff numbers

**Checklist for families:**
1. Confirm current pricing directly with FCC (third-party rates may be outdated)
2. Ask specifically about MemoryCare Home — staffing ratio, wandering protocol, secure environment
3. Request JKM licence number for both main facility and MemoryCare Home
4. Ask about SmartPeep — who is alerted, what is the response protocol?
5. Ask about farm/animal hygiene management for immunocompromised residents
6. Clarify foreign resident deposit terms
