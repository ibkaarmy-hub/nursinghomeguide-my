About

Woon Ho is an owner-operated nursing home in Kempas Baru, Johor Bahru, set on a 21,800 sqft compound with a lawn garden — a real differentiator versus the shophouse-style homes common in JB. The facility is an AGECOPE member and accepts residents with stroke recovery, dementia, Parkinson's, and palliative needs, alongside general elderly residency.

Source: woonhofamilycarecentre.com.my, AGECOPE directory.
