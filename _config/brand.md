# brand.md — Nursing Home Guide Malaysia

## Identity
**Name:** Nursing Home Guide Malaysia (nursinghomeguide.my)
**Tagline:** "Malaysia's most honest nursing home directory"
**Positioning:** A neutral, family-side directory — not run by an operator.

## Who We Serve
Families (adult children, aged 35–55) making one of the most stressful decisions of their lives. Time-poor, emotionally overwhelmed, often searching in both English and Malay. See `_research/user-questions.md` for the specific concerns Malaysian families raise on public forums.

## Credibility Signals
- Published by an independent team of researchers — not an operator with a facility to sell
- Pricing published directly where verified, "Call for pricing" where not — never fabricated
- Bilingual: English + Bahasa Malaysia (BM expansion in progress)
- State-by-state coverage — currently Johor, KL, Selangor; Penang and Perak next

## What We Are Not
- Not a care provider — we are neutral and independent
- Not a lead-generation form farm — we show information first
- Not a government portal — we are practical, not bureaucratic
- Not anonymous about our data sources — every editorial cites verifiable facts

## Public-facing voice (anonymity)
Public site uses a faceless "team of researchers" / "our team" persona. Never name individual contributors on the public site, including the founder. See `_config/voice-rules.md`.

## Visual Identity
- Primary: `#2563eb` (blue)
- Body font: Inter (400/500/600/700/800)
- Tone: Calm, clear, trustworthy
- See `style.css` for the full token set

## What We Never Say
- "Best nursing home" without qualification
- Vague superlatives ("world-class", "premium", "holistic")
- Anything that sounds like advertising copy for a specific facility
- Claims we can't back with a verifiable source
