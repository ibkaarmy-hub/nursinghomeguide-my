# brand.md — Nursing Home Guide Malaysia

## Identity
**Name:** Nursing Home Guide Malaysia (nursinghomeguide.my)
**Tagline:** "Malaysia's most honest nursing home directory"
**Positioning:** The only directory that shows real pricing

## Who We Serve
Families (adult children, aged 35–55) making one of the most stressful decisions of their lives. They are time-poor, emotionally overwhelmed, and desperate for clear information. They often search in both English and Malay.

## Credibility Signals
- Founded by a medical doctor
- Pricing published directly — not "call for quote"
- Bilingual: English + Bahasa Malaysia
- State-by-state coverage across Malaysia

## What We Are Not
- Not a care provider — we are neutral and independent
- Not a lead-generation form farm — we show information first
- Not a government portal — we are practical, not bureaucratic

## Visual Identity (current)
- Primary: `#2563eb` (blue)
- Body font: Inter (400/500/600/700/800)
- Tone: Calm, clear, trustworthy
- See `style.css` for the full token set

## What We Never Say
- "Best nursing home" without qualification
- Vague superlatives ("world-class", "premium")
- Anything that sounds like advertising copy for a specific facility
