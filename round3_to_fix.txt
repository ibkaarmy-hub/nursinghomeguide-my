
========== AY253 pusat-jagaan-warga-tua-sra ==========
Pusat Jagaan Warga Tua SRA was founded on 1 January 2020 by Sharil Hussein, a former Ministry of Health civil servant, as a protective shelter for elderly people discharged or abandoned from hospitals whose families cannot provide care. The home is located in Taman Malawati, Kuala Selangor — a suburban setting roughly an hour from central Kuala Lumpur. Residents are drawn predominantly from referrals through the hospital system rather than private placements, reflecting the founder's healthcare background and the NGO's welfare-first mandate. The facility is operated under the Selangor Relief Agency (SRA) umbrella.

As of early 2025, the home was caring for 34 elderly and frail residents. Funding had historically relied on Selangor Zakat Board support of approximately RM1,850 per resident per month, which was subsequently cut to RM800 — precipitating a financial crisis that led to press coverage about potential eviction from their rented bungalow by May 2025. A local welfare NGO (Mayda) subsequently stepped in to assist in identifying alternative premises. The resident profile appears to include bedridden and uzur (severely debilitated) individuals, suggesting the home handles higher-dependency cases beyond basic assisted living.

The financial turbulence reported in 2025 is a serious consideration for families. Before placing a resident here, it is essential to verify the current premises situation and operational continuity, confirm whether Zakat funding has been restored or replaced, and understand the fee structure for non-asnaf residents. The phone number on record is +60 19-879 1926. Ask specifically about the care team composition, whether nursing staff are available overnight, and what happens to residents if the home faces another funding shortfall.
